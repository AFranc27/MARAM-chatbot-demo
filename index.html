AI Tutor/Chatbot Demonstration

This is an embedded demonstration of the MARAM tutor/chatbot that will be piloted alongside the latest MARAM foundations eLearn by Safe and Equal. Start by clicking on 'Ask AI' and type in a question.

Some sample/suggested questions are:

  • Can you help me understand the responsibilities for a MARAM prescribed worker?
  • What kind of roles are Comprehensive level?
  • What should I do to ensure my organisation is MARAM aligned?
